Who We Are

Crafting outdoor spaces that breathe with your lifestyle.

Terrain & Co. is a full-service landscape company rooted in the belief that the outdoors should be an extension of your home — not an afterthought. We combine horticultural expertise with skilled craftsmanship across every discipline: from grading and planting to pavers, decking, and fine woodworking.

Our team handles projects of every scale, bringing the same obsessive attention to detail whether we're building a sweeping estate garden or refreshing a backyard patio. No job is too small. No vision is too ambitious.

What We Do

Every outdoor need,
one trusted team.

01 🌿
Landscape Design & Installation

Complete landscape planning and planting — from full yard overhauls and garden beds to sod installation, native plantings, and perennial borders that thrive year after year.

02 🪨
Pavers, Patios & Walkways

Natural stone, concrete pavers, flagstone, and brick — we design and install durable, beautiful hardscape surfaces that anchor your outdoor living spaces with character.

03 🪵
Decks & Woodworking

Custom-built decks, pergolas, arbors, raised planters, and outdoor structures — crafted in hardwood, cedar, or composite. Where carpentry meets the great outdoors.

04 🧱
Retaining Walls & Grading

Functional meets beautiful. Our retaining walls and grading solutions solve drainage and erosion challenges while adding bold visual structure to sloped properties.

05 💧
Irrigation & Drainage

Smart irrigation system design, installation, and repair. French drains, catch basins, and grading that protects your investment from erosion and standing water.

06 ✂️
Maintenance & Seasonal Care

Weekly lawn care, seasonal cleanups, mulching, pruning, snow removal, and everything in between. We keep your landscape looking its best 365 days a year.

07 🌳
Tree Service & Removal

Professional trimming, shaping, and removal with full cleanup. We handle everything from specimen trees needing light pruning to full storm-damage recovery.

08 🔥
Outdoor Living Spaces

Fire pits, outdoor kitchens, seating walls, water features, and landscape lighting — we build complete outdoor rooms designed for the way you actually live.

09 💡
Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage LED lighting systems that highlight architecture, illuminate pathways, and extend the usability of your outdoor space long after the sun goes down.
