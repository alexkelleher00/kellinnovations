ROLL FOR 3 MAN
Players take turns rolling until someone rolls a 3 - that player becomes the Three Man to start the game.
SIPS MODE
ON = light mode. When doubles are rolled, you hand the dice - each die given = 1 sip. No rolling or timers needed. OFF = full drink mode with waterfall timers.
DOUBLES RULE
When doubles are rolled, the roller picks who gets each die. Those players drink based on what they roll (or sip if Sips Mode is on).

7 & 11 RULE
Total of 7 = player to the RIGHT drinks. Total of 11 = player to the LEFT drinks. Stacks with other rules.
ROLL AGAIN ON HIT
When a player triggers any rule (3-Man, 7, 11, doubles), they roll again instead of passing. Turn only ends on a clean roll.
DICE OFF THE TABLE
1-in-100 chance: if dice "fall off" the table the roller instantly becomes the new Three Man. Pure chaos mode.
